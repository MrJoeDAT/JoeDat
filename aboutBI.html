Hey there! I’m Joseph, but you can call me JoeDat - the curious explorer of the tech world! My journey began with a passion for discovery that started long before I set foot in school. As a computer science student, I dabbled in various tech fields, but eventually, I found my true calling in the exciting realms of branding and analytics.

My design adventure kicked off even before my academic journey, and I’ve been hooked ever since. I believe that branding is all about storytelling, and as an analyst, I pay close attention to the little details that elevate my designs. I could spend hours or even days perfecting a logo, making sure every curve and color speaks to the essence of the brand. It’s these nuances that set my work apart and bring it to life!

When I’m not busy crafting captivating visuals, I’m honing my business intelligence skills. Throughout my time in school, I dedicated countless hours to mastering this craft, and I’m confident in my ability to deliver insightful reports and engaging dashboards. The beauty of it all? My design skills enhance my storytelling and presentation abilities, creating a seamless blend of creativity and analysis.

So, whether you need a stunning brand identity or a data-driven story, I’m here to turn your ideas into reality. Let’s embark on this journey together, who knows what amazing discoveries await us?
