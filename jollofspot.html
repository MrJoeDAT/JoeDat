Welcome to Nikki Spice

Meet Nikki, her culinary journey began in her grandmother’s kitchen, surrounded by the aromas of sizzling spices and the flavors of traditional Nigerian dishes. With a heart full of passion and a recipe book filled with family secrets, Nikki opened Nikki Spice to share the warmth, vibrance, and tradition of Nigerian food with everyone who walks through her doors.

The Nikki Spice brand embodies warmth, authenticity, and a dash of boldness. With its striking yellow and red colors, the logo radiates energy, instantly capturing the spirit of the brand. The playful, expressive design reflects Nikki’s passion for cooking and her commitment to delivering meals that are as visually appealing as they are delicious. From the comforting taste of Jollof rice to the spicy thrill of suya, Nikki Spice offers dishes loved by locals and newcomers alike.

P.S: Nikki Spice may be a concept restaurant, but its heart and vision are just as genuine, crafted to showcase the power of branding and design in bringing a brand’s story to life.

Nikki

Restaurant Owner

Making Nikki Spice has been an amazing journey in crafting a visual identity that mirrors the heart and soul of Nikki's food. Every design element has been carefully chosen to highlight her commitment to quality, tradition, and the joy of sharing a meal. The color palette of bright yellow and deep red reflects warmth, vibrancy, and spice, perfectly in line with the brand’s essence.

Whether you’re a fan of classic Nigerian dishes or just discovering the cuisine, Nikki Spice invites you to pull up a chair and savor the flavors of home-cooked comfort.
